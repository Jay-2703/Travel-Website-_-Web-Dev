Pinto Art Museum

Immerse yourself in a world of creativity and inspiration at the Pinto Art Museum in Antipolo, Rizal. This sprawling art haven showcases a diverse collection of contemporary Filipino artworks, housed within Mediterranean-inspired galleries and lush gardens.

Daraga Church

Perched atop a hill overlooking the picturesque town of Daraga, Albay, Daraga Church is a symbol of faith and resilience. Built in the 18th century by Franciscan missionaries, this architectural marvel boasts a blend of Baroque and Oriental design elements, adorned with intricate carvings and ornate decorations. Inside, visitors can admire the church's majestic interiors, including its stunning altar and ceiling frescoes.

(99+ Reviews)
Price: ₱3999
Luneta Park

Nestled in the heart of Manila lies Rizal Park, a historic urban oasis named after Dr. Jose Rizal, the Philippines' national hero. Spanning acres of lush greenery, this iconic park serves as a tribute to Rizal's legacy and offers a serene escape from the city's hustle and bustle.
